About

Gemstone Recording Studio was started by Cole Phillips while in his freshman year of college. Starting as a dorm room studio, he recorded other artists at his college.

Now, as a fully operational studio, Gemstone Recording Studio is the go-to spot for any step in the songmaking process (producing, recording, mixing, and mastering).
